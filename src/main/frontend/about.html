About TechChallenge

TechChallenge is an educational prototype to support safer over-the-counter (OTC) decisions in the UK. It guides users through the WWHAM intake, applies safety filters (contraindications, interactions, red flags), and presents clear explanations.

What the app does

  • WWHAM intake: Who, What, How long, Action taken, Medication.
  • Safety filters: Removes options that are unsuitable based on answers.
  • Explanations: Why a product is recommended or ruled out, plus self-care tips.
  • Pharmacy handoff: Encourages pharmacist/GP referral when red flags appear.

Methodology

  • Structured intake aligned to WWHAM.
  • Rule-based mapping from symptom pathways to OTC classes.
  • Red-flag triggers for referral to NHS services or a pharmacist.

Scope & limitations

This tool does not replace professional medical advice. For severe or worsening symptoms, use NHS 111 or seek urgent care as appropriate.

Data & privacy

The MVP does not store identifiable personal data. If accounts/history are added later, a clear privacy notice will be provided.
